Dublin Techmakers

Women Techmakers is Google's global program and brand for women in technology. Dublin Techmakers is a local chapter for women in Dublin. Dublin Techmakers is dedicated to women in technology in Dublin. This page will show events, study jams, workshops interviews, motivational quotes, and more. If you are interested to join, or have any feedback, please reach out to us.
